- lacked ethical judgment
- pattern matching instead of true understanding

## Main Issue

## AI Output Problems

# Bias in AI

## Why Bias Happens

- AI trained on human data
- learns existing biases
- biased training = biased outputs

## Examples

- resume screening bias
- unfair job recommendations
- discrimination between groups
